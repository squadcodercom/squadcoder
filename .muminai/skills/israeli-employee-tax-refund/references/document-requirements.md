# Document Requirements by Refund Trigger

This is the per-trigger document checklist the skill walks the user through in Step 6 of SKILL.md.

## Always required

- Form 106 from every employer for each tax year claimed.
- תעודת זהות (front + ספח).
- אישור ניהול חשבון בנק (bank account confirmation) for the refund payout. Issued by the bank, valid for 90 days typically.

## Per-trigger documents

### 1. Mid-year job change / multiple employers

- All Form 106 documents for the year, one per employer.
- If a תיאום מס was filed mid-year, attach the האישור (it reduces the refund but must be disclosed).

### 2. Partial year / unemployment

- Bituach Leumi annual confirmation (אישור על קצבאות חודשיות) listing months and amounts of unemployment / חל"ת. Order online at btl.gov.il.

### 3. Maternity / paternity leave

- Bituach Leumi דמי לידה annual confirmation.

### 4. Military reserve duty

- טופס 3010 (אישור על ימי מילואים) from the reserve unit's mishak (administrative officer).
- Must list total days served in the relevant tax year.

### 5. Section 46 donations

- Original signed receipts (קבלות מקוריות) from each Section-46-approved institution.
- The institution's Section-46 approval number must appear on each receipt.
- For donations from 1.1.2026 onward: confirm the institution reported the donation digitally to the Tax Authority. Paper alone may not suffice.

### 6. Yishuv mezakeh

- אישור תושבות (residence confirmation) from the local authority, for each tax year, confirming 12+ consecutive months of residence.
- Each year must be confirmed separately.

### 7. New immigrant credit points (Section 35)

- תעודת עולה issued by the Aliyah and Integration Ministry.
- Date of aliyah on the certificate determines the credit-point schedule (3 / 2 / 1 over the first ~3.5 years).

### 8. Section 40g academic studies credit

- Diploma (תעודת בוגר / בוגרת for bachelor's, מוסמך / מוסמכת for master's).
- Transcript or graduation certificate stating completion year.

### 9. Single parent / alimony payer

- Court judgment (פסק דין) establishing single-parent status or ordering alimony payments.
- Bank transfer records confirming the alimony was actually paid.

### 10. Section 9(5) disability

- Medical-board determination (החלטת ועדה רפואית) of qualifying disability.
- Subsequent ratification by the Tax Authority's disability committee (חוקרי נכות).

### 11. Sections 45A and 47 (life insurance and pension)

- אישור הפקדה שנתי from each pension fund, kupat gemel, or life insurance provider.
- Distinguish between employer-side deposits (already in Form 106) and self-deposits (only the latter generate the extra benefit).

### 12. Keren hishtalmut early withdrawal

- תלוש משיכה from the keren showing 47% withholding.
- Date of original opening of the keren (to confirm <6 years).

### 13. Child credit points mismatch

- Updated 101 form for the relevant year showing the correct number of children.
- Custody documents (court judgment) if relevant.
