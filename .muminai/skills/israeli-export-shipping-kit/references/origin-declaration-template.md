# Invoice Origin Declaration Templates

Use these exact wordings. Print on the commercial invoice, packing list, or proforma. Sign manually.

## EU-Israel (and UK-Israel) - Invoice Declaration

Use for shipments up to 6000 euros (any exporter) or for any value if approved-exporter.

> The exporter of the products covered by this document (customs authorization No ...) declares that, except where otherwise clearly indicated, these products are of ISRAEL preferential origin.
>
> [Place and date]
>
> [Signature of the exporter; name of the signatory written in clear]

Replace the authorization number with your approved-exporter number or omit "(customs authorization No ...)" if you are not approved (permitted only up to 6000 euros).

## US-Israel FTA - US Origin Invoice Declaration

For shipments from the US to Israel. Reverse direction (Israel to US) uses a similar declaration adapted for Israeli origin.

> I, the undersigned, hereby declare that unless otherwise indicated, the goods covered by this document fully comply with the rules of origin and the other provisions of the Agreement on the Establishment of a Free Trade Area between the Government of Israel and the Government of the United States of America.
>
> I hereby undertake to provide such supporting evidence regarding the origin status of the goods as may be required by the customs authorities of the importing country.
>
> [Exporter name, address]
>
> [Invoice number and date]
>
> [Manually signed]

Source wording: https://www.trade.gov/us-israel-free-trade-agreement.

## Canada-Israel FTA (CIFTA)

For Canadian exports under the modernized CIFTA (in force September 1, 2019), use Form B239 (Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement Certificate of Origin). Certificate is valid 4 years from signature.

A blank invoice declaration does NOT substitute for Form B239 under CIFTA. Always issue the form.

Source: https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca/publications/dm-md/d11/d11-5-6-eng.html.

## EFTA-Israel

Use the EUR.1 movement certificate or an invoice declaration with the same wording as the EU-Israel declaration (substitute "EFTA" / destination country name in the appropriate box).

## Mercosur-Israel

Use a Mercosur-Israel movement certificate. Invoice-declaration wording is not standard for this FTA; request the certificate via Israeli Customs.

## Signature requirements

- EU, UK, EFTA, CIFTA, Mercosur: manual wet-ink signature required.
- US-Israel: manual wet-ink signature required.
- All: print the signatory's name and position in clear below the signature.

## Record keeping

Keep origin calculations, supplier declarations and copies of each declaration for at least 3 years (EU PEM 2012 rules) or 5 years (US-Israel FTA). Israeli Customs may verify any origin claim with the foreign customs authority.
