# Domain coverage checklist, israeli-mortgage-comparator

Anchor for expert review. Scope: comparing Israeli mortgage tracks across banks, computing mixed-track payments, BoI regulations.

## Must cover (core)
- The 5 tracks (Prime, fixed non-linked, fixed CPI-linked, variable CPI-linked, variable non-linked) and Prime = BoI + 1.5%.
- Current BoI rate / Prime (verify-before-quoting) and that it drifts about every 6 weeks.
- BoI Directive 329: LTV 75/70/50 (foreign 50), at least one-third fixed tracks, at least two tracks, PTI over 40% high-risk flag, Prime+3 stress test.
- Purchase tax brackets (first-apartment 0% to 1,978,745; investment 8%/10%) and the 2025-2027 freeze.
- Amortization math, CPI-linkage on principal, early-repayment penalty by track type, refinancing break-even.
- Required life + property insurance assigned to the bank; closing costs.
- Reservist (Order 8) statutory protections; verify temporary BoI relief frameworks before quoting.

## Should cover (advanced)
- Mortgage advisor vs direct; end-of-quarter negotiation leverage.
- Dira BeHanacha / Mechir Matara discounted-housing lottery.

## Out of scope (explicit)
- Commercial real-estate loans, business credit lines, non-Israeli mortgages (per description).
