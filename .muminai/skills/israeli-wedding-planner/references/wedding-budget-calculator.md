## Quick Budget Calculator

### Formula
```
Total cost = (per_plate_price x guest_count) + fixed_costs
```

## Matana (Gift) Estimation

### Expected Recovery Rate
Typically 40-60% of total wedding cost is recovered through matanot.

| Guest Type | % of Guests | Avg Gift/Person | Expected Total |
|-----------|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Close family | 10% | 750 | guests x 0.1 x 750 |
| Friends | 30% | 400 | guests x 0.3 x 400 |
| Extended family | 25% | 350 | guests x 0.25 x 350 |
| Colleagues | 20% | 300 | guests x 0.2 x 300 |
| Acquaintances | 15% | 250 | guests x 0.15 x 250 |

For 250 guests: ~87,500 NIS in matanot (35% close family and friends tend to give more).
