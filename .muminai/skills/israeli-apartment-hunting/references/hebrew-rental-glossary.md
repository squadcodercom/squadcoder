# Hebrew-English Rental Glossary

## Overview

This glossary covers 80+ Hebrew terms commonly encountered when searching for, viewing, and renting an apartment in Israel. Terms are organized by category for quick reference.

## Property Types

| Hebrew | Transliteration | English | Notes |
|--------|----------------|---------|-------|
| דירה | dira | Apartment | General term for any apartment |
| דירת גן | dirat gan | Garden apartment | Ground floor with private garden area |
| דירת גג | dirat gag | Rooftop apartment | Top floor, sometimes with roof access |
| פנטהאוז | penthouse | Penthouse | Top-floor premium apartment, often with terrace |
| דופלקס | duplex | Duplex | Two-story apartment |
| טריפלקס | triplex | Triplex | Three-story apartment |
| סטודיו | studio | Studio | Single room combining living and sleeping area |
| דירת חדר | dirat cheder | One-room apartment | Small apartment, basically a studio |
| מיני פנטהאוז | mini penthouse | Mini-penthouse | Smaller version of a penthouse, usually duplex at top floor |
| קוטג' | cottage/kotej | Cottage/townhouse | Semi-detached or row house, usually with small yard |
| בית פרטי | bait prati | Private house | Standalone single-family home |
| וילה | villa | Villa | Detached luxury house with garden |
| יחידת דיור | yechidat diur | Housing unit | Legal term for any residential unit |

## Financial Terms

| Hebrew | Transliteration | English | Notes |
|--------|----------------|---------|-------|
| שכירות | sechirut | Rent | Monthly rental payment |
| שכר דירה | schar dira | Rent amount | The actual rent price |
| ארנונה | arnona | Municipal property tax | Paid by tenant, varies by city and zone |
| ועד בית | vaad bayit | Building committee fee | Monthly fee for building maintenance |
| תיווך | tivuch | Brokerage | Broker's commission |
| עמלת תיווך | amlat tivuch | Broker fee | Typically one month's rent + VAT |
| פיקדון | pikadon | Security deposit | Deposit held as security against damages |
| ערבות בנקאית | arevut bankit | Bank guarantee | Bank-issued guarantee (alternative to personal guarantor) |
| ערב | arev | Guarantor | Person who guarantees rent payment if tenant defaults |
| שטר חוב | shtar chov | Promissory note | Written promise to pay (sometimes used as security) |
| צ'קים | chekim | Checks | Post-dated checks (common payment method) |
| הוראת קבע | hora'at keva | Standing order | Automatic bank payment |
| קבלה | kabala | Receipt | Proof of payment |
| מע"מ | ma'am (VAT) | VAT | Value Added Tax (18% as of January 1, 2025; was 17% previously), applies to broker fees |
| הצמדה למדד | hatzmada lamadad | Index-linked (CPI) | Rent increase tied to Consumer Price Index |
