# Incoterms 2020 - Israeli Exporter Cheat Sheet

Authoritative text: ICC (https://iccwbo.org/business-solutions/incoterms-rules/incoterms-2020/).

Incoterms 2020 defines 11 three-letter trade terms. Always write the term AND the named place (e.g. "FOB Haifa", "DAP Berlin, Germany (Incoterms 2020)").

## Any mode of transport (7 rules)

| Code | Name | Seller cost ends | Seller risk ends |
|------|------|------------------|------------------|
| EXW | Ex Works | At seller's premises | At seller's premises |
| FCA | Free Carrier | Delivery to buyer's carrier at named place | Same |
| CPT | Carriage Paid To | Seller pays freight to destination | At first carrier |
| CIP | Carriage and Insurance Paid To | CPT + insurance | At first carrier |
| DAP | Delivered at Place | At named place, not unloaded | Same |
| DPU | Delivered at Place Unloaded | At named place, unloaded | Same |
| DDP | Delivered Duty Paid | At destination, duty paid | Same |

DPU replaced DAT (Delivered at Terminal) in 2020. Under DAP the seller does NOT unload; under DPU the seller DOES unload.

## Sea and inland waterway (4 rules)

| Code | Name | Seller cost ends | Seller risk ends |
|------|------|------------------|------------------|
| FAS | Free Alongside Ship | Alongside vessel at named port | Alongside vessel |
| FOB | Free on Board | On board at named port | On board |
| CFR | Cost and Freight | At destination port | On board at loading |
| CIF | Cost Insurance and Freight | CFR + marine insurance | On board at loading |

## Export vs import clearance

| Incoterm | Export clearance | Import clearance |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| EXW | Buyer | Buyer |
| FCA, FAS, FOB, CFR, CIF, CPT, CIP | Seller | Buyer |
| DAP, DPU | Seller | Buyer |
| DDP | Seller | Seller |

For an Israeli exporter, EXW is the only rule where the BUYER handles Israeli export formalities. Under every other rule the seller handles Israeli customs on the way out.

## Insurance

- CIP requires the seller to purchase insurance at the A level of the Institute Cargo Clauses (all risks).
- CIF requires the seller to purchase insurance at the C level of the Institute Cargo Clauses (limited perils). Buyer may request A level by agreement.

## Typical choices for Israeli exporters

- Container FCL to Europe: FCA Ashdod / FCA Haifa.
- Air freight to the US: FCA Ben Gurion Airport or CIP Los Angeles.
- Small parcels via courier: DAP (buyer handles import) or DDP (seller handles import).
- Bulk sea to Asia: FOB Haifa or CIF Shanghai.
