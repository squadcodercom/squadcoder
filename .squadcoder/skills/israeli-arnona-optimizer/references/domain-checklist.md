# Domain Checklist: israeli-arnona-optimizer

Scope: estimate municipal arnona, check discount eligibility, draft objection (hasaga) letters. Category: tax-and-finance.

## Must cover (core)
- Per-municipality rate structure (zones, usage types, sqm) + the annual national update coefficient.
- Discount eligibility with CORRECT bands: oleh (90%, 100 sqm, 12-of-24-months); soldier (100%); senior by pension/income status (25% / 30% income-tested / 100% with supplement), NOT by age; disabled (medical 90%+ -> 40%, earning-incapacity 75%+ -> up to 80%); low income; student (municipal); single parent (20%); large family (income-tested); bereaved & Holocaust survivor (66%). Primary residence, up to 100 sqm, one discount at a time, annual renewal.
- Objection (hasaga) process with the correct LEGAL deadlines: 90 days to file with the manager, 60 days for the manager to respond, 30 days to appeal (erur) to the committee, then Administrative Court (1976 objection law).
- A working calculator with correct rates, discount bands, and zone systems per municipality.
- Gotchas: per-municipality rate variance, discount renewal/expiry, residential-vs-commercial classification, the appeal deadline.

## Should cover (advanced)
- Payment options (bimonthly, annual lump-sum discount, direct debit, arrears plans).
- Appeal letter drafting with statutory references; surveyor re-measurement; vacant-property partial exemption.

## Out of scope (explicit)
- Income tax, VAT, national insurance (other authorities).

## Authoritative sources
- Local Authorities (Objection to Arnona Assessment) Law 1976; Arnona Discount Regulations 1993.
- kolzchut.org.il (arnona discounts/objection), gov.il property-tax topic, municipal tzav-arnona pages.
