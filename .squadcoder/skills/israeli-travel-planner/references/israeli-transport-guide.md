## Rav-Kav Card
- **Personal**: Requires photo, supports period passes
- **Anonymous**: No ID needed, stored value only
- **Reload**: Train stations, bus terminals, post offices, ravkavonline.co.il, ravpass.co.il, or the Rav-Kav Online / HopOn (Rav-Pass) apps (remote NFC loading)
- **Passes**: Daily, weekly, monthly, cluster-based
- **EMV contactless**: Tap a physical credit card, Apple Pay or Google Pay at the validator, no Rav-Kav needed. Not Egged-only: Dan central-Israel buses since October 2022, Israel Railways at the gates (EMV instead of a paper ticket), and an Egged pilot in Eilat, Jerusalem and Haifa in early 2026.

## Public Transport Fares (Derech Shava reform, effective 25 April 2025)
Single-ride bus fares by distance band (bus only):
| Band | Single ride | Daily cap |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Up to 15 km (urban) | 8 NIS | ~11.5 NIS |
| Up to 40 km | 14.5 NIS | ~21 NIS |
| Up to 75 km | 19 NIS | ~27 NIS |
| Up to 120 km | 19 NIS | ~30.5 NIS |
| Up to 225 km | 30.5 NIS | ~52.5 NIS |
| Over 225 km | 74 NIS | none |

- The up-to-15 km urban ride includes 90 minutes of free transfers in the zone.
- Caps rise with the band (table above); the over-225 km band has no daily cap. Fares are slightly higher when a trip includes Israel Railways.
- Nationwide unlimited monthly (up to 225 km): ~315 NIS.
- Seniors 67+ ride free nationwide (previously 75).

## Shabbat Schedule
- Public transit stops: Friday ~2-3 hours before sundown
- Resumes: Saturday ~1 hour after sundown
- Exceptions: Haifa has limited Shabbat bus service
- Alternatives: Sherut taxis, Gett, Yango
- Yom Kippur: ALL roads closed to vehicles

## National Parks (Rashut HaTeva VeHaGanim)
### Pricing (verify current year at parks.org.il)
- Single entry: Adult ~31-46 NIS at the main tourist sites (Ein Gedi and Banias 31, Masada 37, Caesarea 46, Coral Beach 40), child roughly half; smaller sites lower
- Tourist multi-site cards: Blue (3 sites) ~90 NIS, Green (6 sites) ~130 NIS, Orange (unlimited) ~175 NIS (do NOT cover the Masada cable car)
- Masada cable car: separate charge, NOT covered by the tourist cards. Official entrance ~37 NIS adult (21 child, 19 senior); the cable car does not include entrance, at ~32 NIS one-way (16 child) / ~54 NIS round-trip (32 child)
- Matmon annual subscription: ~181 NIS individual; couple and family tiers cost more (verify at parks.org.il)
- Hours: Typically 8:00-17:00 (summer until 18:00)

### Top Parks by Region
**North**: Banias, Tel Dan, Gamla, Korazim
**Center**: Caesarea, Beit Guvrin, Apollonia
**South**: Masada, Ein Gedi, Ein Avdat, Timna
**Dead Sea**: Ein Gedi Nature Reserve, Qumran

## Driving Tips
- Speed limits: 50 km/h urban, 80-90 km/h rural, 110-120 km/h highway
- Toll roads: Highway 6 (electronic), Carmel Tunnels (cash/electronic)
- Parking: Blue-white curb = paid, red-white = no parking
- Gas prices: ~8.07 NIS/liter for 95-octane self-service (regulated max, May 2026; verify the current monthly cap at gov.il)
